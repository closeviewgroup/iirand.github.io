About the Firm

Built for the transactions traditional institutions cannot execute.

IIRand Capital is a specialist commercial asset finance and capital solutions firm, founded by Craig Ngema — a lawyer, strategist, and architect of high-value transactions. The firm was established to address a critical gap in the market: the inability of traditional financial institutions to deliver fast, flexible, and commercially intelligent funding and structuring for asset-heavy businesses.

Beyond asset finance, IIRand Capital operates as a multi-disciplinary capital platform — spanning corporate finance advisory, private placements, trade finance, and asset management — enabling clients to access, structure, and deploy capital across the full transaction lifecycle.

Unlike conventional lenders constrained by rigid credit frameworks, we combine legal precision, capital markets expertise, and entrepreneurial execution to deliver tailored solutions across machinery, equipment, transport fleets, infrastructure, and cross-border trade.

Engagement

How a mandate moves through the firm.

From first inquiry to deployed capital, our process is deliberate, transparent, and built around qualified counterparties. Every mandate begins with an NDA and ends with a structured outcome.

01
Inquiry & NDA

Qualified counterparty engages the desk. NSNC NDA is executed before any sensitive detail is exchanged.

02
Scoping & Diligence

Legal, commercial, and capital review. We assess the asset, the counterparty, and the structure simultaneously.

03
Structure & Terms

Bespoke transaction architecture — not a product selection. Terms sheet reflects the specifics of the mandate.

04
Execution & Stewardship

Capital deployed, contracts closed, ongoing asset management where the mandate extends beyond the placement.
