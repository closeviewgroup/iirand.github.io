Who We Are

Built for the businesses banks won't touch.

IIRand Capital is a specialist commercial asset finance and capital solutions firm — structured to move fast on complex, high-value transactions that fall outside the risk appetites of traditional lenders.

We operate where deal structures are bespoke, where counterparties are sophisticated, and where speed and execution precision determine whether a mandate closes or collapses.

Our desk underwrites, structures, and deploys capital for asset-heavy businesses across transport, machinery, infrastructure, and cross-border trade. We are not a bank. We are capital deployed on commercial logic.

Speed as Strategy

Qualified deals close in 7–14 days. We prioritise execution velocity because commercial windows don't wait for credit committees.

Structural Precision

Every transaction is custom-structured. We price risk properly, align incentives clearly, and build contracts that hold under commercial stress.

Qualified Counterparties Only

We engage serious operators — companies with real assets, legal capacity, and commercial rationale. Engagement begins with an NDA.

Engagement

How a mandate moves through the firm.

From first inquiry to deployed capital, our process is deliberate, transparent, and built around qualified counterparties. Every mandate begins with an NDA and ends with a structured outcome.

01
Inquiry & NDA

Qualified counterparty engages the desk. NSNC NDA is executed before any sensitive detail is exchanged.

02
Scoping & Diligence

Legal, commercial, and capital review. We assess the asset, the counterparty, and the structure simultaneously.

03
Structure & Terms

Bespoke transaction architecture — not a product selection. Terms sheet reflects the specifics of the mandate.

04
Execution & Stewardship

Capital deployed, contracts closed, ongoing asset management where the mandate extends beyond the placement.
